Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Jane Student on April 1, 2019

Analysis

The website I decided to use was my User Journal because that is the only site I have control over at the moment. I turned on all demographics and checked each blog page to make sure GA had been added to it. In the demographics section, the only information recorded was that all users were from the US. The top channels for acquisition are shown to be 69.2% direct and 30.8% referral. The most popular page on the site seems to be Post 4 which was the restaurant website analysis receiving 20% of total page views and adding up to 9 views in total. Behavior flow seems to start with Post 2 then moves on to Post 1 and the home page.

Thoughts

Some of these results are not what I had expected. I would not have guessed that there would be a more popular post even though I do know that we have peer reviews assigned. The ways in which the people would visit the site whether through a direct link or typing was surprising as well.
