Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Jane Student on April 1, 2019

Content Experiments

So the first step I did to check content experience is run the an audit through Lighthouse and in total my journal received 93 in performance and 87 in accessibility. This was a higher score than I had gotten last time so I was happy to see this. The recommendations showed to give links unique names under accessibility improvements. Another suggestion was under performance to improve color visuals for the blog. For best practice my blog got a total of 86. One area of improvement was a suggestion to make the images all measure to the correct ratio. I did notice that they are a bit big on some of the posts. They sometimes overload the content making it seem uneven with the entry visually.

I was able to get a visitor in the funnel and they went through the social connect network. I definitely think I could have made an easier funnel since I noticed that in total my blog had 6 visitors within the past week. But I am learning as I go through these Google Analytics assignments.

The whole experiment was a first time learning experience for me. Comparing the data of visitors and just how they navigate through the site was a part of Google Analytics I didn’t know existed with the database system. I think I should have made my blog easier to navigate and cleaned it up a bit more before putting up the funnel. GA is something I would use in the future for sure if there comes a time where I have a website for something. Now that I know the basics, these tools will be handy in the future.
