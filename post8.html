Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Jane Student on April 1, 2019

Goals

The goal presented is for Google Analytics to track traffic within a certain page. Is this case it would be my User Science blog. I have created a page in my blog called “Test Page.” This will be the page that will be added to destination goals on GA.

Funnels

Data on GA can be condensed into funnels to get specific information from the goals set. It is almost like a step by step process wear the visitor of a site will be followed by where they go within the site to finally end up at the destination point which is the goal. For example, someone may first land on the home page then look through the site before landing on the tester page.

Results

So far I haven’t had any views on my test page, but did get more traffic in Post 6 for whatever reason this week. I am going to try giving the URL out to others and see if it results in any views and if the funnel begins to work.
